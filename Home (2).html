Dataset Information

OECD Health Statistics from OECDiLibrary

OECD Health Statistics offers the most comprehensive source of comparable statistics on health and health systems across OECD countries, including data found in the publication Health at a Glance. It covers obesity, suicide and life expectancy, health care financing, resources, and utilization, and more, with data dating back to 1960.

Australia Immigration Data from Kaggle

The United Nations Department of Social Affairs provides annual data on international immigrant flows recorded by destination countries, focusing specifically on Australia as part of the International migration flows to and from selected countries - The 2015 revision.

Overseas Migration from Australian Bureau of Statistics

Statistics on Australia's international migration, detailing migration by state and territory, country of birth, visa type, age, and sex.
